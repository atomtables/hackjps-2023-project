Welcome to RateMyTeacher

Ever gotten your schedules and wondered to yourself, "What are my new teachers like?" With RateMyTeacher, you no longer need to wonder! This app allows you to see what other students have to say about your teachers. Better yet, you can leave your own reviews to help out your fellow students! Learn about your teacher's quirks, teaching style, and more!










Features and Possibilities

Community-like system

All reviews are posted publicly for others to see. Others can like it if they feel it is correct, or report it if they feel it breaks the rules. Either way, the people get their feedback.

Simple information update system

Instead of relying on web scrapers or other methods that may be unreliable, RateMyTeachers uses the community to keep information updated. This means all information can be placed accurately.

Tightly knitted to each district

Each city, state, district, or even school, could have their own instance of RateMyTeachers. This allows the app to be personalized and more widespread to the kids that need it, unlike RateMyTeacher, which is global, but unheard of in most places.
